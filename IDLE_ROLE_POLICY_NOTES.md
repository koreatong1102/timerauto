# Idle Role Policy Hotfix

This patch changes no-hit commentary from one-role filler into role-aware broadcast pacing.

- Short no-hit gaps stay silent.
- Medium no-hit gaps are caster calls: brief, factual flow updates.
- Late-round no-hit gaps are caster calls because they are time-pressure calls.
- Long no-hit gaps with gauge damage, recovery context, or one-sided prior exchanges are analyst comments.
- Same-role TTS busy behavior from prior builds is kept.
- Damage, scorecard, long rest summaries, match-end summaries, unicode image loading, and browser round-intro suppression are kept.
